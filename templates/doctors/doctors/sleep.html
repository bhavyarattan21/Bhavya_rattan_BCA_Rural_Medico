Sleep Hygiene Tips

1. Keep a consistent sleep schedule

Go to sleep and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends, to regulate your body's internal clock.

2. Create a relaxing bedtime routine

  • Start 30-60 minutes before bed.
  • Include activities like warm showers, gentle yoga, meditation, soothing music, or reading.
  • Avoid blue-light devices and stressful conversations.

3. Turn off electronic devices before sleeping

Limit exposure to blue light to maintain melatonin levels. Avoid phone notifications that may disrupt sleep.

  • Do at least 30 minutes of aerobic exercise daily.

  • Use meditation or deep breathing techniques.
  • Consider weighted blankets for anxiety relief.
