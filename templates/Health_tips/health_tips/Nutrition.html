Why Eating Healthy Matters

  • Food is your body's fuel.
  • Deficiencies can cause health problems.
  • Too many calories = weight gain = higher disease risk.
  • Quality of diet affects disease risk, longevity, and mental health.

Benefits of Whole Foods

  • Protects against diseases (heart disease, cancer).
  • Promotes longer life.
  • Improves mental health.

Basics of Healthy Eating

  • Focus on nutrient-dense foods (fruits, veggies, eggs, nuts, seeds, fatty fish).
  • Eat a variety of foods to support gut health and prevent disease.
  • Balance meals with carbs, fats, and proteins.
  • Limit ultra-processed foods (sodas, candy, baked goods).

Healthy Grocery List

  • Fresh and frozen fruits and vegetables
  • Protein sources: chicken, fish, tofu, eggs
  • Carbs: canned beans, whole grains, starchy veggies
  • Fats: avocados, olive oil, full-fat yogurt
  • Healthy snacks: nuts, seeds, nut butter, hummus, olives, dried fruits
