Affordable Generic Medicines

What Are Generic Medicines?

Generic medicines are drugs that have the same active ingredients, strength, dosage form, and route of administration as brand-name medicines. They are proven to be just as safe and effective but come at a significantly lower price.

‚úÖ Generic medicines are FDA-approved and contain the same active ingredients as their brand-name counterparts.

Difference Between Brand-Name and Generic Medicines

  • Cost: Generics are typically 30-80% cheaper than brand-name drugs, saving you a lot of money over time.
  • Quality: Generic medicines have the same quality, strength, and dosage as branded drugs, and are rigorously tested for safety.
  • Packaging: The only difference is that generics often come in simpler packaging and are marketed under the chemical name rather than a brand name.
  • Approval: Generic medicines are approved by regulatory bodies like the FDA and are manufactured under the same high standards as branded drugs.

Why Buying Generic Medicines is Okay

  • FDA-Approved: Generic medicines are approved by the FDA and meet the same rigorous safety standards as brand-name drugs.
  • Effectiveness: Generics are just as effective and have the same therapeutic results as branded versions. The active ingredients are the same!
  • Trusted by Doctors: Doctors prescribe generics regularly, knowing they are a safe and affordable option for their patients.
  • Save Money: By choosing generics, you can save a lot of money on prescription costs without sacrificing quality.
  • Widely Available: Generic medicines are widely available in pharmacies and online, making them an accessible option for most people.
